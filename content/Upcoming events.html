Mapping interactively with Rshiny - Christina Buelow


Christina is a quantitative ecologist at Griffith University interested in conserving blue forest ecosystems and informing climate-smart coastal restoration.

In this workshop, she will show us how to use R to read in spatial data and make beautiful interactive online maps.

We will be using the following R packages: rshiny, shinydashboard, sf, mapdeck, leaflet, rmapshaper.

This workshop is suitable for beginners, but some R knowledge will help.
